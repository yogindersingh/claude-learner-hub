# Kai Lens Studio Brand Identity

## Business Essentials
- **Business Name:** Kai Lens Studio
- **Tagline:** "Your story, beautifully told"

## Brand Colors
- **Deep Navy** `#1A1A2E` — headings, backgrounds
- **Warm Sand** `#E8D5B7` — accents, highlights
- **Antique Gold** `#C9A96E` — buttons, links

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond
- **Body Text:** Nunito Sans

## Tone of Voice
Calm, artistic, confident — like a photographer who sees beauty in everything and speaks with quiet authority.

## Brand Vocabulary
**Use:** timeless, craft, story, light, moment, authentic, intentional
**Avoid:** cheap, package, deal, basic, quick, selfie, snap

## Closing Phrases
- "Let's tell your story."
- "See you through the lens."
- "Your moment, preserved."

## How to Apply
When creating any content (emails, social posts, web pages, documents, designs):
1. Use Deep Navy and Warm Sand as primary colors; Antique Gold for CTAs
2. Set headings in Cormorant Garamond, body in Nunito Sans
3. Adopt the calm, artistic tone — authoritative but approachable
4. Favor brand vocabulary over alternatives
5. End with one of the closing phrases when appropriate
